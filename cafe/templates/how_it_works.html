Reinvent Customer Experience

CampSite lets you you customers order and pay through their mobile phones

First, Managers create the digital Restaurant

This means adding the Chefs in turn (activate if currently working), setting up the tables and logging the dishes

  1. Add all the chefs that work in the restaurant and assign them an ID. They will use this ID to work inside the app

  2. Similar to the Chefs, add ID's to the Runner's

  3. Runner's are the personnel that carries orders from the chef to the client

  4. Enumerate each table and seat and add it to the "Table" section

  5. Add Dishes with time to complete, name, description and price.

Clients can access the Restaurant

This means clients can go to CampSite and order from inside your Restaurant

  1. Clients browse from the restaurant's dishes with help from the menu (which showcases the items)

  2. Client orders quantity of item, item and adds a note in case he or she has any special petition

  3. For example: "No Avocado no my toast"

  4. Just when the user sends the dish it automatically appears in the Chef's home page

Chef recieves client orders through tablet

The tablet is provided by CampSite, where (active) Chefs can see orders sent by clients

  1. The order is automatically displayed to the Chef once client orders

  2. Chef's can see client specifications

  3. Once a Chef clicks on the Dish name, the order is eliminated from said Chef's screen

  4. Normally, Chef tablets are positioned in front of each Chef in eyesight

Runners are notified once dishes are ready

Similar to the chefs, Runners are provided a terminal where they can see the dishes that are available to be delivered

  1. Runners also get notified once the client is ready to pay

  2. The runner confirms the dishes by touching the dish name

  3. Runner confirms payment by touching "has payed bill"

  4. Once the Runner confirms a clients payment the client gets a confirmation screen
